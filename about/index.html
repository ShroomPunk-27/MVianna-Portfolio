To whoever's stumbled into this corner of the world, welcome!

I'm MV Art, and this is my workshop. A quiet nook tucked somewhere between the realms of dreams and dice rolls, where characters come to life and fantasy worlds unfold across parchment and pixels.

I'm a digital artist and animation student from Argentina, who specializes in character-driven artwork with a love for cozy aesthetics, fantasy settings, and narrative details. Whether it's through warm lighting, symbolic props, or a subtle smirk on a character's face, I strive to make every piece feel like a story in motion.

Here you'll find the things I've created, from character designs to illustrations, and even some animations. So stick around, poke through the shelves, and maybe you'll spot a familiar face or two (or a hidden key... shhh).

TLDR; Thanks for stopping by! I hope you find something here that makes you smile, inspires your next adventure, or sparks a commission idea ;)
